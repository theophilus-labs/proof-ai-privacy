Privacy Policy – Proof AI

Effective Date: January 2026

Proof AI respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Proof AI mobile application.

Information We Process

Proof AI allows users to upload images or paste text for analysis. This content is processed solely for verification purposes, such as identifying possible manipulation, AI generation signals, or matching text to known sources.

  • Uploaded images and text are processed temporarily
  • No user accounts are required
  • No personal identifiers are requested

Data Storage

Proof AI does not permanently store uploaded images or text unless explicitly stated within the app. Content is processed in-memory or temporarily for analysis and then discarded.

Third-Party Services

Proof AI may use trusted third-party services (such as cloud-based AI or OCR providers) strictly to perform analysis. These services process data only as required to deliver results and are not permitted to use the data for advertising or profiling.

Advertising

Proof AI does not display ads and does not sell user data.

Children’s Privacy

Proof AI is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data during processing. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements. Updates will be reflected on this page.
